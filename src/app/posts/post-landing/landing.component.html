Rambling Runs


A gentle 10km run through spectacular countryside, on flat and easy to medium slopes.

Join our group of ramblers for a 10km jog across some spectacular countryside

There are five classic circuits within a 20km radius of Bromma



  • Forest cycles


    Groups of up to ten are taken on challenging forest routes up to 30km in length

    Full support is provided for mechanical failures

  • Cycling regime


    8:30am - Meet with bike at Forest Fitness carpark in Alsten (bikes will be taken on truck)

    Or alternatively meet at designated location at 9:30am

    Bus to location for arrival at 9:30am

    10am to 12pm Two hour all terrain cycle at location

    Optional picnic lunch (byo). There will be early transport leaving back to FF carpark if you wish



  • Swim


    9:30am - Meet at Lake Malaren

    Buoys at 1km, 2km and 3km will be marked in the lake or in the open water

    Please swim to your ability and a small rescue boat always follows for safety

    In the cooler months (October and November) a wetsuit is recommended

    www.lakes.se lists daily water temperatures

  • Lake or Open water swims


    Groups of up to ten are taken on challenging forest routes up to 30km in length

    Full support is provided for mechanical failures
