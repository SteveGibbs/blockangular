About Forest Fitness

Tired of the gym? Forest Fitness takes you back outdoors to workout with nature.

Four types of classes are offered in amazing natural locations with indoor back ups for when the weather turns bad.

Reinvigorate your senses with rambling runs, functional forest workouts, swim and bike sessions during summer and cross training.


Forest Fitness has been designed to connect you back to nature with medium sized groups of like minded people.

Groups range from 10 to a maximum of 20 in size with two instructors to provide personalised guidance.
